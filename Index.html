12 Incredible Heart Nails to Try This Valentine’s Day

by Maria @SoNailicious on February 12, 2022

Categories: Nail Art, Nailspiration

Valentine’s Day is just around the corner! So here are 12 incredible Heart nails for every taste and skill level to get you inspired. From stylish almond nails with hearts to cute square heart nails, and from flawless hand-painted Valentines Day manicures to super easy glitter heart nails, this compilation has it all to get your creative juices flowing. Enjoy!

1. Rainbow Heart nails

How-to: Use straight line stencils or striping nail art brush to create a two-tone base, then add Love Heart glitters. Finish with glossy top coat.

2. Sideways French Valentine’s Day nail art

How-to: Paint pink tips, outline with white using a thin liner brush, add red heart glitters.

3. Doodles Valentine’s Day manicure

How-to: Paint nails powdery pink, then add tiny doodle hearts using a liner brush. Top coat to finish.

4. Black Heart nails

How-to: Use a fine nail art brush to paint black hearts. Requires patience and steady hand.

5. Euphoria nails

How-to: Edgy Valentine’s nails. Use liner brush to create symmetrical waves and hearts.

6. French Tip heart nails

How-to: Freehand pink French tips, paint red hearts with detailing brush. Finish with glossy top coat.

7. Easy Red Heart nails

How-to: Use French manicure stencils for half-moon base, add red glitter hearts, seal with top coat.

8. Swirly Hearts Valentine’s nails

How-to: Pick pink & red lacquers, use liner brush to create swirls and hearts.

9. Comme des Garçons Heart nails

How-to: Fine detailing brush for miniature hearts, wait between layers to avoid smudging.

10. French Tip and Black Heart nails

How-to: Paint black tips, add black heart stickers. Top coat to finish.
